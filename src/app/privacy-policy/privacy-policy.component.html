Privacy Policy

Wose App is a fitness service powered by team Makka Pakka. You can choose from a catalog of workouts led by expert trainers.

Wose App is designed to protect your information. When you download the Wose, we collect information about your browsing activity, including the workout previews you watch, and about any marketing campaign that led you to the app. We may collect information about your browsing activity in Wose, such as the pages you view, the features with which you interact, and actions that cause you to enter and exit the app such as following an external link that leads you to Wose. We also collect some demographic information including your gender and age range. This information is associated with your User ID and is used to improve the service.


Activity Sharing

In the Wose, your Workout and Activity data will be shared with friends, and Makka Pakka will retain your Workout and Activity data for a short period of time and will use this data only to enable the Sharing feature. This data may include: active calories, exercise minutes, time zone, and workout information such as title, type, and duration.
